Our Mission Statement

The Medford Animal Rescueâ€™s mission is to liberate the lost, abandoned, and mistreated animals and provide for their relief and safety until they are reunited with a loving owner or placed in a new home. At our rescue, we pledge to provide for health, overall prosperity and to never euthanize nor abuse any of the pets we come in contact with. All pets, regardless of their circumstance will never be out of time to find a safe haven. All pets adopted from our shelter are spayed and neutered before they are adopted. We would appreciate your contributions and your willingness to join us in our journey!

Visit Us
